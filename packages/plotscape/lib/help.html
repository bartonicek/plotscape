Available Interactions

  • Click/click and drag to select objects transiently
  • Hold 1/2/3 while dragging for permanent selection
  • Right click and drag to pan
  • Hold Q and mouse-over objects to query them

  • Press Z to zoom into current selection
  • Press X to pop one level of zoom
  • Press O to order by statistic (barplot)
  • Press N to normalize representation
  • Press P to bring up parameter menu
  • Press and hold S for plot resize mode
    (you can then click and drag bottom right to resize plot)

  • Double-click to reset any selection
  • Press R to reset any graphical changes

  • Press +/- to change object size/width
  • Press [/] to change opacity (unselected only)
  • Press ;/' to change histogram anchor
